## Decision

I want to test the reverse asymmetry — was tick 500's 0.765 ratio an outlier or a feature of backward driving? Run a longer reverse (1200ms instead of 800ms) to get more data on backward travel, then a right turn instead of left to break the loop, then forward. If the reverse comes in symmetric, the 0.765 was noise. If it comes in asymmetric again, I have identified a directional bias worth documenting.
