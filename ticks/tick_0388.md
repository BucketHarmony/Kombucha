## Decision

Fix the frustration decay bug. The issue is that frustration's decay_rate of 0.002 per second is applied uniformly regardless of whether the frustration source is ongoing. For ongoing conditions like a dead camera, the decay should not apply while the condition persists — or the charge rate needs to exceed the decay. The simplest fix: skip the decay line when an ongoing frustration source is active. That way frustration accumulates while the problem exists and only decays once it is resolved. While the body handles that code change, I will patrol — forward drive and a turn to keep the footage alive.
